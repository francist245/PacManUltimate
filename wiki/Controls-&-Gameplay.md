# Controls & Gameplay

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Arrow Keys / WASD | Move Pac-Man |
| Enter | Select menu option |
| P | Pause (Classic mode) |
| ESC | Back to menu / Unpause |

## Classic Mode

You are the yellow Pac-Man competing against 3 AI rival Pac-Men and 4 ghosts!

### Objective
Eat as many dots as possible to outscore your rivals. Clear all dots to advance to the next level.

### Scoring
| Item | Points |
|------|--------|
| Dot | 10 |
| Power Pellet | 50 |
| Ghost (while powered) | 200, 400, 800, 1600 (combo) |
| Cherry | 100 x Level |
| Strawberry | 100 x Level |

### Lives
You start with 3 lives. Touching a ghost (when not powered up) costs a life. Lose all lives and it's game over!

### Power Pellets
Eating a power pellet turns all ghosts blue and scared for 7 seconds. During this time you can eat them for bonus points. The combo multiplier doubles with each ghost eaten per power pellet.

### Fruit
Bonus fruit appears after eating 70 and 170 dots. Grab it quickly — it disappears after 10 seconds!
