# Tips & Tricks

## Classic Mode Strategies

### Beating the Rivals
1. **Go for power pellets early** — they're worth more points AND let you eat ghosts for big combos
2. **Watch CHOMPER** — the orange rival specifically targets dots near you, so keep moving
3. **Let rivals take risks** — they can get caught by ghosts too, giving you a 5-second advantage
4. **Clear corners first** — rivals tend to cluster in open areas; corners have uncontested dots

### Ghost Survival
1. **Learn the scatter pattern** — ghosts retreat to corners every 7 seconds; use this time to eat safely
2. **Tunnel is your friend** — ghosts slow down in the side tunnels
3. **Don't chase scared ghosts too far** — the 7-second timer is tight; prioritise nearby ghosts
4. **Clyde's safe zone** — Clyde retreats when you're within 8 tiles, so hang near his corner

### Power Pellet Combos
1. **Wait for ghosts to cluster** — eat the pellet when multiple ghosts are nearby
2. **Combo multiplier doubles** — 200 → 400 → 800 → 1600 for each ghost per pellet
3. **Max combo per pellet** — eat all 4 ghosts for 200 + 400 + 800 + 1600 = 3000 points!

### Level-Specific Tips
- **Neon Arena (L2):** Use the open centre to bait ghosts, then escape through side corridors
- **Shadow Maze (L3):** Learn 2-3 safe loops you can repeat; the winding paths trap ghosts too
- **Fire Fortress (L4):** Thick walls mean fewer escape routes — always have a plan B
- **Ice Gauntlet (L5):** Grab power pellets aggressively; they're your lifeline in tight corridors

## Ghost Tag Tips
1. **Predict, don't chase** — cut off Pac-Men at intersections
2. **Use walls** — herd them into dead ends
3. **Focus one at a time** — don't spread your attention

## Pellet Frenzy Tips
1. **Never stop eating** — keep the combo alive
2. **Circle the maze** — systematic routes eat more pellets than random movement
3. **Chain ghost-eats** — eat a pellet, eat a ghost, repeat for massive combos
