# Minigames

## Ghost Tag

**You play AS a ghost!** Chase and catch AI-controlled Pac-Men before time runs out.

### Rules
- **Time Limit:** 30 seconds
- **Targets:** 3 AI Pac-Men running around the maze
- **Points:** 500 per Pac-Man caught
- **AI Behaviour:** Pac-Men run away from you — they get smarter as you get closer!

### Tips
- Corner them against walls
- Cut off escape routes rather than chasing directly
- The maze has no dots — pure chase action!
- Purple-themed walls for a spooky vibe

### Controls
Same as Classic mode — Arrow Keys / WASD to move your ghost.

## Pellet Frenzy

**Every dot is a Power Pellet!** Non-stop ghost-eating chaos.

### Rules
- **Time Limit:** 20 seconds
- **All dots are Power Pellets** — every one you eat scares the ghosts
- **6 Ghosts** on the field (Red, Pink, Cyan, Orange, Purple, Green)
- **Combo System:** Each pellet increases your combo multiplier
- **Eaten ghosts** respawn at random corners

### Scoring
- Power Pellet: 50 x Combo
- Eating a scared ghost: 200 x Combo
- Build massive combos by chaining pellets and ghost-eats!

### Tips
- Keep moving — never stop eating pellets
- Chain pellet-eats to keep ghosts scared permanently
- Green-themed walls for a Matrix-like feel
- Watch out — if a non-scared ghost touches you, it's game over!

### Controls
Same as Classic mode — Arrow Keys / WASD to move.
