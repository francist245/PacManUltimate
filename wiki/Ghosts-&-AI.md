# Ghosts & AI

## The Four Ghosts

Each ghost has a unique AI personality, just like the original Pac-Man!

### Blinky (Red) — "The Shadow"
- **Behaviour:** Directly chases Pac-Man's current position
- **Danger Level:** ★★★★★
- **Strategy:** Always on your tail. Use corners and tunnels to shake him.

### Pinky (Pink) — "The Ambusher"
- **Behaviour:** Targets 4 tiles AHEAD of Pac-Man's current direction
- **Danger Level:** ★★★★
- **Strategy:** She tries to cut you off. Change direction suddenly to throw her off.

### Inky (Cyan) — "The Unpredictable"
- **Behaviour:** Uses both Pac-Man's position AND Blinky's position to calculate a target
- **Danger Level:** ★★★
- **Strategy:** Hardest to predict. Keep moving and don't let Blinky get close.

### Clyde (Orange) — "The Shy One"
- **Behaviour:** Chases Pac-Man when far away, but retreats to his corner when close
- **Danger Level:** ★★
- **Strategy:** Least dangerous but can surprise you when he switches modes.

## Ghost Modes

Ghosts alternate between two modes:
- **Scatter** (7 seconds) — Each ghost retreats to its home corner
- **Chase** (20 seconds) — Ghosts actively hunt Pac-Man

## Frightened Mode
When you eat a Power Pellet, all ghosts turn blue and move randomly for 7 seconds. Eat them for massive combo points!

## AI Rival Pac-Men

Three AI-controlled Pac-Men compete with you for dots:

### SPEEDY (Cyan) — "The Greedy"
- Always chases the nearest dot
- Fast and efficient dot-eater
- Avoids ghosts when possible

### MUNCHY (Green) — "The Explorer"
- Targets clusters of dots in unexplored areas
- Roams far from its starting position
- Great at clearing distant corners

### CHOMPER (Orange) — "The Aggressive"
- Deliberately targets dots near YOU
- Tries to steal dots from your path
- The most annoying rival!

All rivals can be caught by ghosts too — they respawn after 5 seconds.
