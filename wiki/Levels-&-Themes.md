# Levels & Themes

PAC-MAN ULTIMATE features 5 handcrafted levels, each with a unique maze layout and colour theme. After completing all 5, levels cycle back with increased ghost speed.

## Level 1: CLASSIC
- **Walls:** Blue
- **Background:** Black
- **Layout:** The original Pac-Man maze design with symmetrical corridors and the classic ghost house in the centre.
- **Difficulty:** Easy — wide open paths, good for learning.

## Level 2: NEON ARENA
- **Walls:** Neon Green
- **Background:** Dark green tint
- **Layout:** Open centre arena with tight corridors around the edges. More room to manoeuvre in the middle but watch the corners!
- **Difficulty:** Medium — the open centre is a double-edged sword.

## Level 3: SHADOW MAZE
- **Walls:** Purple
- **Background:** Dark purple tint
- **Layout:** Winding labyrinth paths with lots of turns. Easy to get cornered by ghosts.
- **Difficulty:** Medium-Hard — learn the escape routes or get trapped!

## Level 4: FIRE FORTRESS
- **Walls:** Orange/Fire
- **Background:** Dark red tint
- **Layout:** Thick walls creating strategic corridors. Fewer paths means more head-on encounters with ghosts.
- **Difficulty:** Hard — limited escape routes, plan your path carefully.

## Level 5: ICE GAUNTLET
- **Walls:** Ice Blue/Cyan
- **Background:** Dark blue tint
- **Layout:** Narrow corridors with extra power pellets to compensate. Every path feels like a gauntlet run.
- **Difficulty:** Very Hard — tight spaces, fast ghosts, but plenty of power pellets.

## Level Cycling
After Level 5, the mazes repeat but ghosts get faster each cycle. Can you beat the high score?
